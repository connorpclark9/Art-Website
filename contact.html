Apply to Join Kindred Seal

We're always looking for talented local artists whose work and story deserve a wider audience. If you believe your work is a fit for Kindred Seal, we'd love to hear from you.

We look for artists who demonstrate quality and originality in their craft, have a compelling story or perspective that enriches their work, and show a genuine commitment to their creative practice.

What to Include

Send an email to connorpclark9@gmail.com with:

  • Your name and location
  • A link to your portfolio or website
  • A brief statement about your work and practice
  • 5-10 sample images of recent work (as attachments or a shared link)
Suggested subject line: Artist Application: [Your Name]

All inquiries: connorpclark9@gmail.com

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about discovering art and working with Kindred Seal.

We review every application personally, evaluating artists on the quality and consistency of their work, the originality of their voice, and the depth of their story. Not every applicant is accepted — that selectivity is what ensures the quality of our roster.

Browse our artist profiles and available work. When something speaks to you, email us at connorpclark9@gmail.com. We'll answer questions, provide additional images if needed, and arrange the purchase directly — no impersonal checkout process.

We provide high-resolution images and detailed descriptions for every piece. If an in-person viewing is important to you, reach out and we'll do our best to arrange something depending on the artist and location.

Shipping is handled per purchase to ensure each piece is packed and delivered with care. Costs vary by size and location, and we'll provide a clear estimate before you finalize.

Yes, some of our artists are open to commissions. Get in touch and we'll connect you with an artist whose style aligns with your vision. Availability, timelines, and pricing vary by artist.

Quality, originality, a compelling story, and genuine commitment to your practice. We want to represent artists who have something meaningful to say through their work and are willing to collaborate on telling that story.

We handle professional storytelling, your dedicated artist page, buyer connections, ongoing marketing through our journal and social channels, and more! Our goal is to free you up to focus on creating.

No penalty or pressure. Art finds its audience on its own timeline. We will continue to actively connect your work with potential buyers.
